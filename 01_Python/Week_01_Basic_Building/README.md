# Basic Building  

1. Variables.  

2. Mutable and Immutable objects.
    - list.
    - strings.  

3. Operators.
    - arithmetic -> + , - , * , / , % , // .
    - logical -> and , or , not .
    - comparsion -> < , > , <= , >= .
    - bitwise -> & , | , ~ , ^ , << , >>.  

4. Conditional statements.
    - if , elif and else.
    - is , in , == , != .  

5. Looping 
    - for(start,stop,step).
    - while(condition).
    - while/for else .
    - loop contols -> break , continue and pass.
